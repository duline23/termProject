My time in Rwanda

I spent extensive time in Kigali, Rwanda this summer with members of my church. We were fortunate enough to meet with the United States Liaison for UNICEF in Rwanda, the Archbishop of the Baptist dioces, and several other officials who were able to provide valuable insight into their beautiful naiton. Every month, on the last Saturday, the whole nation comes together to clean up the country. From repaving all the dirt roads to picking up all litter, the nation selflessly comes together as a community to better their conutry. Amongst several other respectable traditions, I am passionate about letting others know of their core values of selflessness, community, and love. I intened on taking a gap year between college and graduate school and living in Rwanda.
